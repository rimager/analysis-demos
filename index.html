About

This website was created by three BFA Graphic & Web Design program students and professor Rima Gerhard at Miami International University of Art & Design. The website is available on desktop and mobile, providing users with information regarding the fairs and galleries during Miami Art Week 2016. Other sections include additional information about restaurants and areas of interests, along with a link to the City of Miami Trolley information for these events. We hope you enjoy this website and find it useful!

Ericka Gracia

(BFA) Graphic Design

I am a proud Latina with Salvadorian and Mexican heritage, currently pursuing a career that will allow me to create solutions for my clients and their brands. I am a versatile creative designer with a passion for branding, my desire to create innovative designs is my drive. Most of my work is influenced by mixed media, I enjoy combining techniques such as hand-lettering and motion graphics.

Luis Escobar

(BFA) Graphic Design

I am a Colombian creative designer that specializes in motion design, web design and print graphics. My main influences include sacred geometry, humanist philosophies and the tattoo industry. I have a background in civil engineering and art history, subject matters that continue to feed my designs and artwork.

Rima Gerhard

Instructor, Web Design

I am an instructor at the Miami International University of Art & Design. I have dedicated myself Web Design & Development because it allows me to combine my Engineering background with the aesthetics of front end design.
